1. Generate and plot the following signals in time domain and also sketch its amplitude and phase
spectrum. Verify the result:
Impulse
Unit Step
Exponential
Unit ramp
Sinc
Rectangular



clc;
clear;
close all;

fs = 1000;              % Sampling frequency
t = -1:1/fs:1;          % Time vector
N = length(t);          % Number of samples
f = (-N/2:N/2-1)*(fs/N); % Frequency axis for spectrum
x = zeros(size(t));
x(t==0) = 1;     % Approximate impulse

figure;
subplot(3,1,1);
plot(t,x);
title('Impulse Signal');
X = fftshift(fft(x));

subplot(3,1,2);
plot(f, abs(X));
title('Amplitude Spectrum');

subplot(3,1,3);
plot(f, angle(X));
title('Phase Spectrum');
